Konark Sun Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage Site

The Magnificent Heritage

Built in the 13th century by King Narasimhadeva I, the Konark Sun Temple stands as a masterpiece of Kalinga architecture.

The temple represents the chariot of Sun God with 24 wheels, pulled by seven horses, showcasing extraordinary architectural brilliance.
