Taj Mahal

The Ultimate Monument of Love

A Love Story in Marble

Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, the Taj Mahal stands as the finest example of Mughal architecture.

This ivory-white marble mausoleum took 22 years to complete (1632-1653) and employed over 20,000 artisans.
